About Us

Playing netball improves coordination, fitness, confidence, team building skills and is a great opportunity for meeting new friends and socialising. These are just a few of the benefits of one of the fastest growing female sport in England! Many schools and clubs offer netball for children in upper primary school years and above currently in England, however we believe that the benefits of netball can be enjoyed by children in lower primary school years too. The Netball Hub is a community for parents and caregivers who wish to engage their children in netball, by providing free activities and ideas to do at home.

Below you can find some simple drills and ball skills to try with your little ones at home, a quiz you can do with your children to spark their interest in the sport, and also information on the Superleague in case your children are keen to watch netball games in action!

Netball Skills

The video shown here is from England Netball's 'Bee Netball' programme. The video is aimed at under 11's and includes simple ball drills that can be done at home for children aged 5+.

Parents can watch the video and then try some of the activities out with your child in the garden, or watch the video together and then choose the activities you'd like to try.

Learning simple ball skills, such as catching and throwing a ball, helps to develop good hand-eye coordination, as well as quicker reaction times. These videos encourage you to be creative with the equipment you have at available at home, and don't require a netball or netball post.

If you enjoy trying out these skills with your children, you can find more videos from Bee Netball by visiting the Bee Netball page on the England Netball's website.

Superleague Teams

Did you know there are ten teams in the Vitality Superleague Netball team? A great way to boost chidren's interest in the sport is to watch it played professionally and see role models they can aspire to and support. Sky Sports actually shows many of the matches played in this league, so you can tune in and watch live netball together when the next season begins!

You can view the locations of all the teams currently in the superleague on our handy map, if you live close by you could even get tickets to go and watch a game together. Simple click on one of the locations on the map and you will see the team and a link to their website for more information.
